Art

Abstract Art

Learn new art skills in this class. This is a four part series that is limited to eight people. All supplies will be provided for this Tuesday class.

Vision Boards

Frame your future instead of your past. Whether you have already completed your board or just beginning — join us on Wednesdays for show and tell, fun, and inspiration!

Cooking as Nutrition Therapy

Did you know that Medicare may cover a visit to your nutritionist for certain health conditions? Learn more while joining us for a delicious cook‑along class. Recipes will be sent out the week of class.

Dance

Beginning Line Dance

No experience is needed to attend this class. Dances are broken down into easy‑to‑learn steps.

Education

Creative Writing

This is a six week class that takes a deeper look at a variety of poems.

Spanish with Friends

No experience is needed and all levels are welcome in this friendly group. This class uses the free Duolingo website. Come and go as you please — with no worries if you need to skip a class.

Aging Commission Resources

ACR is a leader in assistance, advocacy, and answers on aging. Learn more about the wide variety of resources they offer: caregiving, counseling, Medicare, Social Security, and more.

Exercise

30 Minute Core

The easy movements presented can be modified to your fitness level. This program uses standing, sitting, and optional floor exercises.

SAIL (Stay Active and Independent for Life)

An evidenced‑based fitness program that can be done while sitting or standing. This program incorporates cardio, balance, strength, and flexibility.

Beginning Tai Chi or Tai Chi 2

Tai Chi is great for balance, stress relief, and challenges your brain to learn something new. Tai Chi 2 will build on what was learned initially and teaches new forms.

Upper Body Mobility
